Preface

This book is adressed to several kinds of person:

  • Users, who wish to quickly grasp how things are done in ooc

  • Compiler writers, who want to know what they should implement!

  • Critics, who like to read, emit judgement, and do nothing about it

It is assumed, throughout the book, that you have a basic knowledge of programming. If you come from a C or Java programming background, you should feel at home.

I hope you'll enjoy the read![1]
