Chapter 1. Installation

  • Download the release for your platform from one of the mirrors at http://ooc-lang.org/download

  • Follow the instructions in the INSTALL file for your distribution.

Often, the installation is as easy as extracting the archive and adding ooc/bin to your PATH, or making a symbolic link to ooc/bin/ooc in /usr/bin/
